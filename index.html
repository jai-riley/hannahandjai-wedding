Arrive & Check In

Taco Bar Dinner

Kick off the weekend with a taco bar. Chicken yucatan, ground beef, all the fixings — rice, beans, salsa, the works. Gluten-free and halal options available.

Bagels, muffins, cinnamon buns, fresh fruit, yogurt, turkey sausage (halal), and hard boiled eggs. Coffee, tea & chai included.

Send-off breakfast with pancakes, scrambled eggs, turkey sausage (halal), fresh fruit, and hashbrowns. Gluten-free pancakes available. Coffee, tea & chai included.

Safe travels home! Check out of your cabin by 11:00 AM. It's been a weekend to remember.
